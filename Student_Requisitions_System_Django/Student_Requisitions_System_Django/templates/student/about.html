Answering A Few FAQ


What is Digital Student Requisition System?

The Digital Student Requisition System is an automated way to apply for student requisitions and collect them at the office after completion without having to do the tedious manual task of approaching every faculty and submitting it to the respective department

Why use this system?

Completely Online! Saves Paper Work and time. Gives real time status of the requisition application. Efficient! We can even apply for requisition even on a holiday! This system is qually important for the digitizing world of today! Can be integrated into a wider system of the college

What are the advantages of this System?

It saves a significant amount of time for both students, teachers and different sections. Students need not wait for different teachers to approve their requests, and teachers can relax when having a busy schedule.

Is this system suitable for implementation?

Ofcourse Yes! we have carefully crafted this system for the purpose of implementation at college level! We have built a error free system for successful implementation
